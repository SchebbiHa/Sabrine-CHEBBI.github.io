About Me

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at Inria Lille, France, affiliated with the CRIStAL Laboratory and the Scool research team. Prior to this, I held a postdoctoral position at AIMS South Africa and was a member of the Decision Making group within the Computer Science Department at the University of Tübingen, Germany.

I completed my PhD at the Nonlinear Analysis and Geometry Laboratory (LR21ES08), under the supervision of Ahmed Bchatnia and Makram Hamouda.
